About us

Socialgorithm aims to provide an environment for people to learn about and explore their knowledge of computer science and artificial intelligence through alternative mediums to the classroom, such as workshops, hackathons and competitions.

We maintain an open source platform that can be used by anyone to host a workshop or competition. We also provide guidance on running a successful event.

We are a non-profit community interest company, any surplus contributions not used towards platform hosting costs or organising events are either reinvested into serving Socialgorithm's mission of furthering computer science education, or used to reward the developers and organisers that improve the platform or run events.
